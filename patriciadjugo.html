Original Characters

2021

PERSONAL ART - Original Characters

Date Created: August 2021
Medium: Digital art

Inspired by yokais, I designed a trio based of the kappa, yurei, and tanuki. The three characters are a set based on the square, triangle, and circle shape, telling about their shape language. The colors used also tells how they are linked, for instance, the red being used in some areas of the characters.

Night's Moment

2021

ISOMETRIC ART - Night's Moment

Date Created: October 2021
Medium: Adobe Illustrator

This isometric art I did was for a school assignment I had where we were asked to create an isometric design centered around a product. It centers around the nighttime theme with all the objects that are related to it. My experience with watching television shows at nighttime frequently in the past is what inspired me to create this piece. The key feature in this artwork is the branch popping out of the television as sometimes when we watch television, the programs that are televised pops out in our imaginations, which that tree branch represents.
